📐 Project Work: Exploring Plane Surfaces

Let's discover how school geometry brings the real world to life!

⏱️ Total Time: 30 Minutes 🎯 3 Objectives

Project Objectives

  • Identify: Find everyday household objects with flat (plane) surfaces.
  • Understand: Discover geometric boundaries by physically tracing object edges.
  • Connect: Match classroom geometry with your surrounding environment.

Step 1 Introduction & Object Collection (5 Mins)

What is a Plane Surface?
A plane surface is a completely flat surface that can extend forever in all directions, or it is simply the flat face of a solid object.

Your Task: Find 5 items within your immediate reach that have completely flat faces. Check them off as you find them:
