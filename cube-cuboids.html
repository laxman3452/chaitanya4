Mathematics: Cubes & Cuboids

Class 4 | Lesson Day 2

1. Revision (10 Mins)

Let's look back at what we know:

  • Plane Surfaces: Flat surfaces like a sheet of paper.
  • Solid Objects: Objects that take up space (3D).
Teacher's Note: Check students' homework drawings of plane shapes.

2. Introduction (15 Mins)

Cube

● Has 6 equal square faces.

Examples: Dice, Rubik's Cube

Cuboid

● Has rectangular faces.

● Opposite faces are equal.

Examples: Book, Pencil Box, Brick

3. Activity: Observe & Compare (15 Mins)

Feature Cube Cuboid
Faces Equal square faces Rectangular faces
Measurements Same length, breadth, height Different measurements
